FAQ'S OF SHIKSHA SETU APP

What is Shiksha Setu??

Shiksha Setu is a digital school management system portal. It is a one stop platform to access school details around the state. It works as a register for teachers for smooth enrolment and monitoring of their students

Why Shiksha Setu?

Shiksha Setu is built dynamically to overcome the challenges faced in UDISE, maintained by the Ministry of Education, Government of India. Some of the challenges Shiksha Setu fixed are as follows: (a) It helps analyse student-teacher ratio at the base level. (b) Possible amalgamation of schools through the portal. (c) Predictive analysis of teachers retirement in namely periods of 3/6/9/12 months. (d) Daily attendance capturing feature through AI enrolled face recognition. (e) Student absentee count along with their parents number for better monitoring, etc.

What is the benefit of Shiksha Setu App?

The Shiksha Setu App will help build a direct connection between teachers and students. The portal also helps to filter teachers based on their strengths and weaknesses, which in turn will help students to get the best education. Apart from the educational perspective, the portal also helps to monitor infrastructure requirements, teachers pay-slips, free textbooks, scholarships, vaccinations, free uniforms, etc.

How will Shiksha Setu App help the schools?

The Shiksha Setu App will largely help the Principal/ Headmaster and teachers in devoting more time in class room activity. From syllabus to uniform details, everything will be available on the portal so that no teachers or headmasters will have to keep running for the basic information. Schools can raise a request in the portal which will be answered in as soon as possible. This way the commute and communication become more transparent thereby building a better place for the students

How will Shiksha Setu help parents?

The Shiksha Setu portal helps students to transfer at any level. They can apply to any good institution withing the state by using the dropbox concept in the Shiksha Setu App. The portal can also generate the on-line transfer certificate for the students.

How will Shiksha Setu help the department?

The Department of school education can get accurate up-to-date figures of enrolled students for the distribution of free textbooks, uniforms, and grants, etc. These figures are also used for conducting Gunotsav. The portal will also help the department analyse the number of vacant posts of requirements and rationalizing of teachers.

Why do we get different geo-locations in different devices?

Different devices show different geo-locations because there are several factors which affects the accuracy, different devices have different hardware component as well as the software, also network comaptibility plays a major role in determining very accurate location.However, in Shiksha Setu app the geo-location capturing has been made available in offline mode.

Why do we get the spoof detected error in portrait mode?

Whenever the AI detects a foreign object or a person while detecting the face of a particular teacher or student, It shows "spoof detected".However in most cases, the enrollment of students were done without proper lighting, or with multiple faces in the background or the picture was taken from very far away.Hence, it is advised to follow the enrollment guidelines, so that these issues wont arise in the future

Why does the app sometimes display yesterdays data?

This is mainly because yesterdays data was not synced properly, it is advised to maintain the syncing and downloading process daily, and also it is advised to logout and login once to solve this.

How do we display names of students in landscape mode?

Sometimes because of the faulty enrollment of students, the AI faces difficulty to capture the faces of certain students while taking group attendance.Hence , it is advised to find out the faulty enrollments and re-enrol them.So that this issue gets resolved.

Why does the location show distance error above 750m or below that?

If the location error is above 750ms then the only possible solution is to re-capture the location from Samsung tablet only and let the location settle in for 30-60 seconds before capturing it,after capturing the location it is advised to take the attendance in offline mode. If the location error is below 750ms then the solution is to uninstall the application first, then install it again. After installing, login ,then clear the apps data, and logout , then login again and capture the location again like mentioned above, then take the attendance in offline mode.

Why does it take more time to take attendance in some faces as compared to others?

The newly updated Shiksha Setu App has enabled a learning mode while taking attendance in portrait mode which will teach the A.I. to learn faces of those students that takes more time than others while taking attendance. By taking attendance through learning mode for 2-3 times A.I. will then automatically detect those faces easily in landscape attendance

Why do we have a download pop-up every time we try to take attendance?

The download is for syncing and updating the attendance and names of students. You can take the attendance offline and then after or before start of class, with a good network you can enable the downloading online.

What is the standard photo format for enrolment of students?

The student photographs should have a clear background without any other student in the frame, adequate light, open eyes and only his face (not body) while enrolling.
